Looking after your children’s teeth from a young age is the best first step to setting them up for a lifetime of good oral health.
What to expect at your child’s dental check-up
At your child’s dental check-up we will
1. Assess any medical implications of your child that may affect their dental health
2. Examine the growth and development of your child’s bite, jaws and crowding
3. Check for cavities and gum problems
4. Check for tongue positions, breathing issues, and other oral habits that may detrimentally affect your child’s growth
5. Give you advice on how to care for your child’s teeth, including a detailed lesson on brushing and flossing tips
6. Discuss your child’s diet that is relevance to oral health
7. Explain the use of fluoride and oral health products that your child may need

If a tooth or several teeth are extracted or missing, it is now possible to replace them using dental implants. Dental implants are a fixed, long-term tooth replacement option that recreate a complete functional tooth, and looks just like a natural tooth.
An implant is composed of two main parts: One part is the titanium implant body that takes the place of the missing root, and the second part is the tooth-coloured crown that is attached on top of the implant.
Among dental replacement options, dental implants are considered to be the most natural-looking replacement for missing teeth. Whether tooth loss was caused by decay, injury, gum disease, oral defects, or other issues, dental implants can provide a durable replacement. Unlike removable tooth replacement options, dental implants can deliver unparalleled security and stability, which reduces the risk of gum irritation and eliminates movement.

Removal of a tooth may be required for multiple reasons, including a cracked tooth, a root fracture, deep decay, gum infection, trauma or for orthodontic reasons.
At Evergreen dental care, our dentists have vast experience in safe and effective tooth extractions. With effective and adequate local anaesthesia for maximum comfort, a caring touch and the use of sedation techniques if needed, we aim to get the best possible outcome for you.
Following a dental extraction, you will be provided with comprehensive follow up care by your dentist, and we will always make ourselves available to ensure your healing period goes as smoothly as possible.
Contact our caring team at Evergreen Dental Care to arrange an assessment for you or a member of your family regarding tooth extractions.

Natural, healthy teeth do vary in colour between individuals. As people age, it is common for teeth to become dull and yellow. Many dietary factors can also contribute to discolouration and staining of teeth including smoking, red wine, tea, and coffee. Fortunately, stained, and yellow teeth don’t have to stay that way.
