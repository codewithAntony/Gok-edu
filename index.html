Evergreen Dental Care

We are a locally owned dental practice established by husband and wife team, Dr Nyaga and Dr Mukami. At Evergreen Dental Care, the focus of our efforts is you. We treat our patients like we would our own family. Our mission is to provide high quality dental services in a caring and friendly environment with careful attention to detail and deep personal respect. We strive to serve the community with a focus on affordable, lifetime family dental care so that together we can create something to smile about.

Our Services

General Dentistry

From prevention to rebuilding, replacing and removing teeth. Our dedicated team can help you to achieve optimal oral health.

Dental Implants

Dental Implants are a highly successful, fixed, and natural looking solution to replace missing teeth or a loose denture.

Braces

Straightening your teeth not only transforms your smile but can also improve your oral health, general health and self-confidence.
